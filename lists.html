TENSES
  1. Present Tense
    • Present Simple Tense
      1. Helping verb
        • DO and DOES
        • Usages
          • DO=you,i,we,they and plural names
          • DOES=he,she,it and any singular name
    • Present Continous Tense
      1. Helping verb
        • IS,AM and ARE
        • Usages
          • ARE=you,we,they and plural names +4th(ing)
          • IS=he,she,it and any singular name +4th(ing)
          • AM=I
    • Present Perfect Tense
      1. Helping verb
        • HAVE and HAS
        • Usages
          • HAVE=you,i,we,they and plural names
          • HAS=he,she,it and any singular name
    • Present Perfect Continous Tense
      1. Helping verb
        • HAVE BEEN and HAS BEEN
        • Usages
          • HAVE BEEN=you,i,we,they and plural names +4th(ing)
          • HAS BEEN=he,she,it and any singular name +4th(ing)
  2. Past Tense
    • Past Simple Tense
      1. Helping verb
        • DID
        • Usages
          • DID=you,i,we,they and plural names he,she,it and any singular name + 2nd form of verb
    • Past Continous Tense
      1. Helping verb
        • WAS and WERE
        • Usages
          • WERE=you,i,we,they and plural names
          • WAS=he,she,it and any singular name
    • Past Perfect Tense
      1. Helping verb
        • HAD
        • Usages
          • HAD=you,i,we,they and plural names he,she,it and any singular name
    • Past Perfect Continous Tense
      1. Helping verb
        • HAD BEEN
        • Usages
          • HAD BEEN=you,i,we,they and plural names he,she,it and any singular name
  3. Future Tense
    • Future Simple Tense
      1. Helping verb
        • WILL and SHALL
        • Usages
          • WILL=you,they,he,she,it, plural and singular names
          • SHALL=i and we
    • Future Continous Tense
      1. Helping verb
        • WILL BE and SHALL BE
        • Usages
          • WILL BE=you,they,he,she,it, plural and singular names+4th(ing)
          • SHALL BE=i and we +4th(ing)
    • Future Perfect Tense
      1. Helping verb
        • WILL HAVE and SHALL HAVE
        • Usages
          • WILL HAVE=you,they,he,she,it, plural and singular names
          • SHALL HAVE=i and we
    • Future Perfect Continous Tense
      1. Helping verb
        • WILL HAVE BEEN and SHALL HAVE BEEN
        • Usages
          • WILL HAVE BEEN=you,they,he,she,it, plural and singular names +4th(ing)
          • SHALL HAVE BEEN=i and we +4th(ing)
